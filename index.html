REvamp isn’t just another student club it’s a movement.
‍
Started by second year students who got tired of waiting for “the right time” and chose to build, learn, ship, and earn from day one.
‍
We’re not alumni. We’re not professionals.
We’re just second-year students who got tired of sitting through tutorials and decided to do something real.
Coders. Designers. Founders.
Trying. Failing. Launching. Learning.
And doing it all in public.
‍
‍We’re building our portfolios out here not inside classroom walls. This is where you get mentored, challenged, and launched into real-world projects and startup ecosystems not some certificate graveyard.
‍
‍REvamp was built by the founders of REstart a student-led initiative that’s already helped 300+ juniors figure out the right college path for them, not just chase some “top college” tag.
‍
We’re still figuring it out ourselves but we’re building a community where no one has to do it alone.
‍
‍We started early, so you don’t have to start late.
